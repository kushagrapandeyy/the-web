## Overview

The startup pitch and concept is for a potential start up idea I had last year in the summer. I had utilized my engineering and design skills to pitch this ride share application, which would be functioning on a standard minimal platform fees and earn the bulk through advertisements physically on the car and inside accessories. This would basically enable us to keep the cost of the rides low, allowing consumers to ride for less and drivers to earn more and have more control over what ads they would like on it.

## Role

**Designer and presenter - solo project.** Responsible for the pitch narrative, slide design, and app wireframes.

## Tools

- Figma - wireframes and layout
- Presentation software - slide design and deck structure

## What Was Designed

### Pitch Deck

A structured presentation communicating the problem, solution, target user, and product vision to a non-technical audience. Each slide was designed with deliberate hierarchy - the problem statement leads with impact, the solution frames the product clearly, and the user persona makes the audience care before seeing any screens.

Key slides included:
- Problem framing with supporting context
- Solution overview with a clear value proposition
- Target user persona
- Product concept and core use cases
- Go-to-market framing

### App Wireframes

Low-fidelity wireframes illustrating the core user flows of the proposed app - enough fidelity to communicate the product concept and interaction model without committing to full visual polish. Wireframes covered:

- Onboarding flow
- Core feature screen (primary value delivery)
- Key secondary states and navigation

The wireframe decisions prioritised clarity of function over aesthetic detail - the goal was to make the idea legible, not prototype-ready.

## Outcome

A complete pitch package - narrative deck plus supporting wireframes - demonstrating the product concept to stakeholders with no prior context.
