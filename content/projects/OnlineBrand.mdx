Building a fragrance brand focused on premium Oudh perfumes. We're still in launch phase, but the work involved is more than just marketing—it's about creating a product people actually want and building a community around it.

**What we're working on:**
- Product development and sourcing authentic Oudh
- Building the brand identity and positioning
- E-commerce platform (launching soon)
- Community and content strategy
- Figuring out logistics, supply chain, and unit economics

It's different from pure software engineering, but the problems are similar: how do you build something people value, communicate why it matters, and scale sustainably. The constraints are just physical instead of digital.
