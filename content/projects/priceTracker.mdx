## Real-Time Price Intelligence at Scale

Grocery Price Tracker is a full-stack database management and price monitoring application designed to track grocery prices across different supermarkets in real-time.

## Database & Performance Optimization

- **PostgreSQL Database**: Designed the relational schema and normalized tables for prices, items, and retail stores. Optimized database performance to process over **10,000+ daily price updates**.
- **Sub-200ms Queries**: Delivered basket price comparisons in under **200ms** by implementing smart caching, indexed aggregations, and query optimization techniques.
- **Indexed Aggregations**: Applied composite and partial indexes on frequently queried fields like store items and prices, allowing lightning-fast data retrieval over large datasets.

## Scraping & Alerting Infrastructure

- **Robust Scraping Engine**: Engineered an automated scraping pipeline to scrape and ingest product prices across **50+ stores** concurrently on a scheduled cron sequence.
- **Smart Caching**: Built a custom Redis/in-memory caching layer to store transient store prices and reduce database workload during peak search hours.
- **Real-Time Alerts**: Integrated an asynchronous alerting pipeline that monitors price changes and instantly notifies users of drops in their watched grocery baskets.
