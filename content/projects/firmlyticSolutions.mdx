## The Problem

Legal practice in India runs on scattered documents. Case files, precedents, matter notes, contracts—all sitting in folders. Attorneys spend 30-40% of their time searching for relevant information instead of doing actual legal work. There's no centralized system that tracks who accessed what, when, and why—critical for compliance and professional liability.

## Solution

Built a full-stack platform with React frontend and Node.js backend running on AWS. Implemented intelligent document management with search, audit trails, and role-based access control. The system processes documents asynchronously, handles concurrent users, and maintains compliance with Indian legal requirements.

**Key Achievements:**
- Scaled to 500+ daily active users in beta
- Processing ~50 documents per day
- Improved attorney productivity by ~30% (less time searching, more time on actual legal work)
- Audit trail system critical for professional liability insurance compliance

**Stack:** React, Node.js, AWS (Lambda, DynamoDB, S3), Cloudflare, TypeScript

**Current Status**
MVP launched with 3 law firms in beta. System handles concurrent users without degradation. Positive feedback on audit trail accuracy and search capabilities.
