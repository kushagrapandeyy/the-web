Worked on the core infrastructure and web presence for SS Pandey & Associates. This involved building out their website, a microservice for secure file access and delivery, and tooling for their marketing and CRM workflows.

**What I built:**
- Website showcasing their practice areas and making it easy for people to get in touch
- Backend microservice handling secure document storage and delivery
- CRM tools to help manage client interactions and leads
- Integration between public website and internal systems

**Tech:** Next.js for the site, Node.js microservices for the backend, secure document handling, cloud storage integration.
